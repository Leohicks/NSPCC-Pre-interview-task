Hi NAME,

The increased occurence of reports and harrowing submissions on the 'Everyone's Invited' website shows that Harmful Sexual Behavior (HSB) is a prevalent and growing concern amongst children.

Yet teachers have raised their voices about the lack of knowledge, training and support provided for relationships education.

We estimate that around a third of HSB is by other children and young people.

As a leader in safeguarding children from HSB we invite you to explore our free online training resource for teachers - Talk Relationships

We've developed the service in partnership with 140 schools across the UK and Channel islands.

Raising students' awareness of harmful behaviours in relationships, their rights and how to access support is the first step in stopping HSB.

Kind regards,
Talking Relationships Team
